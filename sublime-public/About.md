### Sublime Plants is a hardware/software project with the mission to "demystify and democratize plant care systems and knowledge." Our flagship device is an open-source, research grade, transparent and accessible soil-moisture sensor.

---

Plants are a remarkable, yet often overlooked resource. They provide us with food, medicines, textiles, building materials, and much more. In healthy, bio-diverse ecosystems they offer countless ecological benefits and produce zero waste. Despite this, our relationship with them is increasingly fragmented and industrialized.

In a world where capitalistic thinking prioritizes profit and efficiency over wellbeing, and institutions seek to hold all the cards, our goal is to empower individuals and communities with the systems and tools to grow plants with confidence. Even without years of experience. To build local resilience against a fragile, broken system that is increasingly stacked against them. To choose sustainable practices over unsustainable ones.

We believe that knowledge is power, and we aim to accomplish our goal with an open-source hardware ecosystem, and open-core software platform, so that individuals, communities, and professionals alike can understand precisely how they came to their conclusions rather than relying on a company saying, "Just trust us and buy our solution."
